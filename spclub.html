    Swimming

    Swimming can be an excellent hobby — and workout — for people of all ages and fitness levels. It’s low-impact, builds strength and fitness, and is fun. Swimming is an activity that involves coordination of arm and leg motions to propel your body through water. Those motions demand a lot of effort to overcome water’s natural resistance, meaning you’ll certainly strengthen your muscles when you swim. But swimming is primarily a form of cardiovascular exercise which keeps our body fit.

    When you swim for exercise, you get a total-body workout, which means that most of your muscles are involved, which can improve muscle strength. There are four strokes used in swimming: backstroke, butterfly, breaststroke, and freestyle. Each style requires different muscles to work to varying degrees. And our club is offering a swimming club so that you and your friends can both learn as well as practice swimming both for a competitive way and a friendly way!



    Badminton

    Badminton is a racket sport played by either two opposing players (singles) or two opposing pairs (doubles), who take positions on opposite halves of a rectangular court that is divided by a net. Players score points by striking a shuttlecock with their racket so that it passes over the net and lands in their opponents' half of the court. A rally ends once the shuttlecock has struck the ground, and the shuttlecock may only be struck once by each side before it passes over the net.

    When you sweat with a happy heart you build up a healthy you. Badminton is a fun, active sport which is an easy game to practice with few painless rules. It boosts up your muscles, adds strength to the muscles, improves blood flow rate and the benefits are endless. Apart from physical health benefits there are mental advantages of playing badminton too.



    Cricket

    Cricket is played with a bat and ball and involves two competing sides (teams) of 11 players. The captain who wins the toss decides whether his team bats or bowls first. If they bat first , their aim is to score a lot of runs and make sure the other team does not reach that score.

    It is cricket which rules over the hearts of everyone. It creates a lot of excitement and frenzy amongst the fans of the game. It is a highly prestigious and respected game famous all over the world. Cricket is a professional outdoor game played by many countries on a national and international level. We are offering cricket as well as a club so come and join us!



    Football

    Football is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players each, who primarily use their feet to propel a ball around a rectangular field called a pitch. The objective of the game is to score more goals than the opposing team by moving the ball beyond the goal line into a rectangular-framed goal defended by the opposing team. Football is a game that millions of people around the world play and love. It can be called a universal game because every small and big nation plays it.

    Moreover, it’s a great relaxer, stress reliever, teacher of discipline and teamwork. Apart from that, it keeps the body and mind fit and healthy. It’s a team game that makes it a more enjoyable game as it teaches people the importance of sportsmanship. Leadership, and unity.
